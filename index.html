Common Questions

Everything you want to know

What if I lose my phone — will my data be gone? +
Not at all. All your sales, udhar, stock and customer data is backed up to the cloud in real time. Just download the app on any new Android phone, log in with your number — your entire shop is restored instantly.
What if the internet stops in my shop? +
KiranaPOS is built offline-first. Billing, stock updates, udhar entries — everything keeps working without internet. The moment your Jio or Airtel comes back, all data syncs automatically. Your shop never stops.
Can my staff use it on their own phones? +
Yes. Any staff member can log in with their own PIN on any Android phone. They see the same live shop data and can bill customers instantly — same stock, same prices. Admin controls (reports, settings, udhar) stay locked behind your PIN.
Is it really free? What's the catch? +
The first 25 Founding Members get 100% free lifetime access — no subscription, no hidden fees, ever. In return, we ask for honest feedback to help us build better. After 25 spots are filled, a ₹99/month plan will launch.
Why is this not on the Play Store? +
We are in the Founding Member phase — verifying each user personally so we can give real support. Play Store listing is coming soon. For now, install directly from the APK — it takes under a minute and is completely safe.
